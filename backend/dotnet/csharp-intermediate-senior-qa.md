# C# Intermediate and Senior QA Interview Questions

### General C# Questions
1. Explain the difference between `abstract` classes and interfaces in C#.
2. What is the purpose of the `using` statement?
3. Describe how garbage collection works in C#.

### Advanced Topics
1. What are delegates and events? Provide examples.
2. How does asynchronous programming work in C#? What is `async` and `await`?
3. Discuss the role of LINQ in C# applications.

### Scenario-Based Questions
1. How would you handle exceptions in a C# application?
2. Provide an example of implementing a Singleton pattern.
3. What are generics and how do they improve type safety?
